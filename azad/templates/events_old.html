SOCULT NIGHT

Socult Night was an initiative taken by Mr. Himanshu Kanodia in 2017 to strengthen the bonding among junior batches and let everyone enjoy moments of fun and happiness during tiring times. The purpose of Socult Night is not to compete against each other and win the coveted "Best Wing Award", but to enjoy, encourage and make memories to be be cherished. The junior wings are divided into teams and each teams performs a certain set of events. The best performing wing is given the award of best wing.

ILLUMINATION

Illumination (also popular as Illu) is a tradition followed by every hall of residence at IIT Kharagpur pytduring the Diwali festival every year. Large grid structures (called chatai) made of bamboo stripes are used as a framework on which wire loops are mounted to support earthen lamps, known as Diyas. The Diyas form a pattern based on a theme decided by the respective student hall. The event is attended by students from different halls of residence, alumni, faculty, and guests of honor from outside the institute. Judges are selected from amongst the faculty and take multiple routes to visit the different halls, starting at the Technology Students' Gymkhana (TSG).

KHOJ

The onset of July brings with itself new life to the IIT campus. Blooming with it come the innocent first years, unknowing and curious to explore the beautiful campus. Their wish is fulfilled by the Azad hall of residence by organizing KHOJ, a campus treasure hunt for inspiring the first years to explore the campus with friends as a game and make it one of the most memorable event of their stay in IIT KHARAGPUR. It was started in 2000. KHOJ is the first and the biggest event organized exclusively for the first years.
