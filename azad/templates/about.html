About

Azad Hall of Residence is named after India's first education minister, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad. It is famous for having started the tradition of Illumination, or ILLU as today's KGPians know it. Azad Hall is always reputed for its stellar performances in Soc-Cult events, especially Dramatics and Choreography.

Details

  • Founded: 3rd March 1954
  • Motto: प्रणिपातेन परिप्रश्नेन सेवया
  • Warden: Prof. Nikhil Kumar Singha
  • Assistant. Warden(s):Prof. Hemakesh Mohapatra (Mess)
    Prof. Bibhudatta Sahoo (Maintenance)
  • Hall President: Mr. Saurabh Nagarkoti
  • SSM: Mr. Yogender Arya
  • Capacity: 600+
  • Sharing: Single, Double, Triple

There are a total of 424 rooms for the accommodation of the students of Azad Hall, with 260 Single rooms, 20 Double sharing rooms, 142 Triple sharing rooms and 2 Four sharing rooms. Also, the Hall provides 2 Double-bedded AC guest rooms with television and attached bathrooms having geyser installed.

Culture

Azad Hall of Residence is the only Hall in IIT KGP to have a Hall Anthem and an Oath of Fire.

  • Hall Anthem is composed by 1982 alumnus Anup Verma (Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering).

  • Oath of Fire is composed by 2020 alumnus Ashutosh Bhaskar (Department of Geology and Geophysics).

Book of Records

"Records are made to be broken".

  • Azad Hall of Resicence holds the record of bagging the 1st position for five consecutive years (from 2014-15 to 2018-19) in Choreography

  • Azad Hall of Residence bagged the 1st position in Thermocol and Clay Modelling for two consecutive years (2017-18 and 2018-19)

  • Azad Hall of Residence secured record points in Sports and Games - General Championship 2018-19.

  • In 2021, the Weightlifting team created history by securing 27 points, the most by any team till date.

  • Highest ever points attained by any hall in the history of GC Technology.

Night Canteen

Azad Night Canteen, also known as Ravi Da's Canteen or simply Ravi Da, is the main canteen of Azad Hall of Residence. It usually opens in the evening around 9 pm and remains open till 3-4 am. Major employees of the canteen are Manoj, Praveen and Ajay, apart from the owner Ravi. Contact:- 9641384253

SAM D

Sam D refers to a general purpose shop located in Azad Hall of Residence. The shop is located at the mouth of corridor leading to B, C, D and E wings. The shop provides all general utility items like stationery, chocolates, chips, along with edible items like fruits, patty, and juice. The shop is named after its owner.

CYCLE REPAIRING -ASHOK DA

Hall Council Members

Hall Council members are selected each year by the warden of the hall to ensure smooth conduction of hall related activitiies within the hall. These activities include Hall maintenance, welfare activities, grand dinners, festival functions, alumni meets, etc. Hierarchically, the Hall President and Second Senate Member(4th years) work in coordination with the General Secretaries(3rd years) and the secretaries(2nd years) to ensure the smooth functioning of the hall.

Hall Captains

To represent Azad Hall of Residence in the Gymkhana General Championships, every year the Hall Council Members appoint Captains of each event held in the General Championships. The Captains(4th/5th years) work closely with the vice-captains(3rd years) and secretaries(2nd years) throughout the event starting from selection of the event team to ensuring all activities of the event are conducted smoothly and timely. Captains bear the responsibility that best of the members of hall pertaining to the corresponding event participate in the event and perform their level best in the event.
